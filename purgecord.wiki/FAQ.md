# FAQ

**Can I delete other people's messages with this?**
Only if you have the permissions in a server (mod). Otherwise just your own.

**Will I get banned for this?**
Possible. It's self-botting and against Discord's ToS. With reasonable delays it stands out less, but there's always some residual risk. Your call.

**Does it work in DMs and group chats?**
Yes.

**Can I do multiple channels at once?**
Yes, separate multiple channel IDs with commas and it works through them one after another.

**Can I clear my entire Discord archive with this?**
Yes, through the JSON import. You request your data archive from Discord, import the `messages/index.json` and Purgecord goes through the channels. See [JSON Import](Import-JSON).

**Is this the same as Undiscord?**
It's based on it, but fixed and updated because the original stopped working on newer Discord versions.

Sure, see CONTRIBUTING in the repo.
