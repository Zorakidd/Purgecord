# Channel ID

The ID of the specific channel or DM you want to clear.

Click **current** next to the field while you're in the channel and it grabs it.

## Multiple channels at once

You can put several channel IDs in here separated by commas. Purgecord then works through them one after another in a batch. Handy if you want to clean out a bunch of channels in one go without babysitting each one.
